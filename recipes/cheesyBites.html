Cheesy Bites Recipe



Description

These tasty snacks are cheesy, bacony, and easy to make!


Ingredients

  • 1 can Pilsbury Grands Biscuits
  • 8 oz. bag Shredded Colby Jack Cheese
  • 1 package bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 1 egg, scrambled
  • Everything Bagel Seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°.
  2. Cut biscuits in half, depth-wise. Roll out to 1/4" thickness.
  3. Spoon 1 tablespoon cheese into center.
  4. Spoon 1 teaspoon bacon into center.
  5. Pinch biscuits closed and place (seam-side down) into cast iron pan, in concentric circles.
  6. Brush egg over tops of bites and sprinkle with Everything seasoning.
  7. Cook for 28 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
