Spaghetti Recipe



Description

A classic dish!


Ingredients

  • 1 box spaghetti
  • 1 jar pasta sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Salt and boil water.
  2. Once water is boiling, add spaghetti to pot.
  3. Cook for nine minutes.
  4. Drain.
  5. Add spaghetti to bowl.
  6. Pour sauce over spaghetti.
  7. Dump entire contents of parmesan cheese container onto pasta.
  8. Enjoy!
